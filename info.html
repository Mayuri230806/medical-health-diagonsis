Medical Health Diagnosis System

This project uses Machine Learning techniques to predict the possibility of diseases based on the patient's health information. It helps users understand potential health risks quickly and supports early medical consultation.

🩺 Disease Prediction

Predict diseases using patient details such as age, gender, BMI, smoking, physical activity, alcohol consumption, and other health-related factors.

🤖 Machine Learning

The prediction model is trained using medical datasets and Machine Learning algorithms to provide accurate disease predictions.

❤️ Health Awareness

The system encourages users to monitor their health and consult doctors at an early stage whenever necessary.
